Long-Term Vision: Building AGI

Aayush's ultimate long-term goal is ambitious and clearly stated: working towards building his own AGI (Artificial General Intelligence).

What This Goal Represents:

AGI—artificial intelligence that can match or exceed human intelligence across a wide range of cognitive tasks—is considered by many to be the ultimate challenge in AI research. Aayush's goal to build his own AGI indicates:
- Ambition to work on fundamental, transformative problems
- Desire for deep technical mastery across multiple AI domains
- Long-term thinking and patience (AGI is likely decades away from realization)
- Entrepreneurial spirit (building "his own" rather than just contributing to others' work)

Current Focus as Stepping Stone:

Recognizing that AGI is a long-term goal, Aayush has identified a current focus area that serves as a stepping stone: sentiment analysis and AI-human comprehension. He explains: "for now most projects are sentiment analysis based because I want to work towards understanding and comprehension between AI and humans."

This strategic focus reveals several insights:

1. Logical Progression: Understanding how AI can comprehend human emotions and communication is fundamental to building systems that can genuinely interact with and augment human intelligence

2. Practical Application: Sentiment analysis is an established field with real-world applications, allowing Aayush to build expertise and contribute value while working toward his larger goal

3. Foundation Building: Communication and understanding between humans and AI is one of the key challenges in developing AGI—systems need to understand human intent, context, and nuance

4. Current Capability Alignment: His projects (Bloom Mind for mental health assessment, Finnalyze using sentiment analysis for stock prediction) already reflect this focus

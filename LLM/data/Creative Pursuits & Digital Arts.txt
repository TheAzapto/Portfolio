Creative Pursuits & Digital Arts

Beyond his technical skills in programming and AI/ML, Aayush Dutta is a multi-talented creative individual with extensive experience in digital arts, design, and multimedia production. His creative journey began early and has developed alongside his technical skills, making him a well-rounded creative technologist.

Digital Art Journey

Aayush's introduction to digital art began in Grade 8 when he was first exposed to the basics of GIMP (GNU Image Manipulation Program). This initial exposure sparked his interest in digital design and image manipulation. From that point forward, Aayush became a self-taught digital artist, learning through experimentation, online resources, and continuous practice.

His self-taught approach has given him a deep understanding of design principles, color theory, composition, and digital workflows. Rather than following a structured curriculum, Aayush learned by doing, tackling real projects and solving creative challenges as they arose.
